Clinicians using Canadian Accuro EMR can now access Heidi's AI scribe fully integrated into the platform. The integration lets providers complete patient visit documentation with AI while staying within their existing Accuro workflows, and the AI scribe can access selected EMR chart data to generate notes with relevant patient context.
