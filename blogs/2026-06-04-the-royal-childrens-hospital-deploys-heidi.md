The Royal Children's Hospital in Melbourne partnered with Heidi to reduce administrative documentation burden on its clinical staff. Using AI-powered documentation automation integrated with Epic EMR, the partnership enables paediatricians to remain engaged with patients during consultations rather than splitting focus between care and paperwork. Early adoption shows strong uptake, with one clinical champion completing over 600 sessions using the platform.
